About Xinia

I have been collecting Disney dolls for over 20 years. My collection consists of approximately 100 unique dolls. I collect all varieties of Disney dolls from the hard-to-find limited edition dolls, to the more common classic collector's dolls. I have dolls from the early 90s to the present day collections. I am always looking for new dolls and will post the one's that most interest me in the "Doll's Currently Needed" section of this page. Please contact me if you have a doll you think I may be interested in.
